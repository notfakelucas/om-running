Shipping policy

1. Coverage area

We ship throughout Spain and to selected countries in Europe. Some remote regions may have longer transit times.

2. Order processing

Orders are processed within 1 to 3 business days. Cash on delivery orders may require an additional verification step via phone or email before shipping.

3. Delivery time

Estimated delivery is 2 to 12 business days from the moment your order leaves the warehouse. This window is an estimate, not a guarantee: customs delays, severe weather, or carrier strikes do not trigger automatic refunds.

4. Costs and fees

  • Free shipping above €60. Below that threshold, a flat shipping fee applies at checkout.
  • Rejected cash-on-delivery shipments forfeit the original shipping cost and customers may be charged for return logistics.
  • Customs duties and VAT for international shipments are the customer's responsibility.

5. Order tracking

You will receive a tracking number by email as soon as your order ships. Carrier systems can take up to 48 hours to display the first scan.

6. Address accuracy

Customers must provide an accurate delivery address and a valid phone number. Incorrect details or unjustified cash-on-delivery refusals may incur additional logistics fees and result in being blacklisted from future cash-on-delivery orders.

7. Lost or delivered packages

A package is considered lost after 60 calendar days from the shipping date. Packages marked as delivered are not eligible for refunds — please verify with neighbors or the local carrier before contacting support.

8. Multiple shipments

To optimize delivery times, a single order may arrive in separate packages with different tracking numbers.

9. Support

Email support@omrunning-es.com · Monday to Friday, 9:00–18:00 UTC/GMT.
