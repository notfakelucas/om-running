Privacy policy

Last updated: 2026.

At OM Running we take the privacy of our customers seriously. This document explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and the rights you have under GDPR.

1. Data we collect

When you place an order, we collect the data essential to process it:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Delivery address
  • Phone number
  • Order history

Payment data is processed by certified payment systems (Stripe, PayPal). Card numbers are never stored on our servers.

2. How we use your data

We use your personal information to:

  • Process and ship your orders.
  • Contact you about shipping status, especially for cash on delivery.
  • Provide customer support and handle returns or refunds.
  • Meet our legal and tax obligations.
  • Detect and prevent fraud.

3. International transfers

Your data may be processed in international distribution centers and through logistics partners. Every transfer is governed by GDPR-compliant contractual safeguards.

4. Security

We use SSL encryption, restricted internal access, and continuous monitoring to protect your data against unauthorized access.

5. Your rights

Under GDPR, you can access, correct, request deletion, or revoke marketing consent at any time. Write to support@omrunning-es.com.

6. Third-party sharing

We share data only with the partners required to fulfill your order: couriers and logistics partners, payment operators, and regulatory authorities when required by law. We never sell your data for marketing purposes.

7. Cash on delivery

Your phone number is required to verify order authenticity and to enable the courier to coordinate delivery with you.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related question, write to support@omrunning-es.com.
