Returns & refunds

1. Return window

Customers may request a return within 14 calendar days of receiving their order.

2. Product condition

To be eligible for a return, the product must be:

  • Unworn (never used outdoors) and undamaged.
  • In its original packaging with all tags, security seals, and accessories.

Items showing sole wear or visible creases on the upper are not eligible.

3. Prior authorization

Mandatory: contact us before returning any product. Unauthorized returns will be rejected and shipped back at the sender's expense.

4. Refund processing

Once your return is approved and received, refunds are processed within 7 to 14 business days. Original shipping costs and logistics fees are non-refundable.

5. Cash on delivery

Cash-on-delivery orders constitute a binding purchase contract. Customers must accept the delivery with the exact amount ready. Unjustified package refusal results in return shipping costs charged to the customer and possible blacklisting.

6. Damaged or non-conforming items

Report any damage or non-conforming product within 48 hours of delivery, with photographic evidence, to support@omrunning-es.com.

7. Contact

For any return question, write to support@omrunning-es.com.
